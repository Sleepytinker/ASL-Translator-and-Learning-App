CopyNCheck

In our tech-savvy world, plagiarism has become a common practice. It's not uncommon to find acts of plagiarism in places of learning (schools, universities,etc) or during competitions and contests. Moreover, owing to the rise in mass media and influx of knowledge all around us, be it the internet, social media or news it is often that people may unknowingly pass off someone else's original ideas as their own.
While there are many plagiarism checking websites available online, they often cost money, or greatly limit their usage, a hinderence to many.
Hence, it is my aim to attempt and build a similar anti-plagiarism checker that allows users to gauge their work against other submissions, or the internet for free.
